Parallax Scroll

Parallax Scroll is a jQuery plugin to create elements with background images that behaves as if their background-attachment property is between scroll and fixed, similar to the parallax scrolling effect you see on Spotify and is loosely based on Peder Andreas Nielsen’s Parallax ImageScroll.

It makes clever use of background-position and background-size properties instead of CSS3 tranforms and supports IE9 and above. It also runs smoothly on mobile devices due to its simplicity.

Initialization & Options

To initialize the plugin, call the parallaxScroll method on your background elements.

The plugin accepts one option – friction. It should be a float value that is more than 0 and less than 1. A value of 1 causes the background to behave as it has background-attachment: scroll (which is useless). A value of 1 (maximum friction) is the same as background-attachment: fixed.
